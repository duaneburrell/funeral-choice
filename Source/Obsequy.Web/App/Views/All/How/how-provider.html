How Does It Work?

Bid on service requests from families in need.

Though there are similar online services, Funeral-Choice is the first of its kind and strives to create the largest marketplace for facilitating memorial arrangements. Funeral-Choice is designed to streamline the funeral arrangement decision-making process between families and funeral homes.

Funeral directors need only to register their funeral home, create a profile by simply using profile editor. Once this is completed, the profile and the profile’s contact person will be alerted to available opportunities to serve families who desire to arrange memorials in your area, the moment the arrangements are made.

If your funeral home is selected by a family, they will pay 10% of the agreed upon cost for the services they request to Funeral-Choice.com via our website as deposit. The balance of the agreed upon amount will be submitted to your Funeral Home directly from the family.
