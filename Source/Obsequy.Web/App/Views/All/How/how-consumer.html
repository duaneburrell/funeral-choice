How To Make Funeral Arrangements and Save

There are a lot of tips people will give you when choosing a funeral. But they almost always require you to settle for a memorial you or your loved one may not have wanted to sacrifice your funeral and burial traditions or worse. Helping to plan meaningful affordable funeral services is what we do. So before you make a decision to either spend thousands of dollars more than is needed or to limit the memorial so that it fits in a budget, we urge you to try Funeral-Choice.

Get Started

Plan arrangements in the comfort of your own home

Plan or preplan, the meaningful affordable funeral ceremony of a loved one in the comfort and privacy of your own home. We are here to help you and your family make the arrangements which best meet your religious and cultural desires as well as your budget.

Submit - and let us do the searching for you

We take the funeral arrangements you make and find high quality reputable funeral homes willing to provide what you've requested at the best available prices. We save you the time of shopping around and the frustration of negotiating price so that you can focus on healing.

Choose - the funeral home that best fits your request

Within 24 hours, we will email you regarding the funeral homes that have agreed to provide the goods, services and ceremony you have requested. We only ask that you make a 10% deposit to confirm the arrangements and that's it!
